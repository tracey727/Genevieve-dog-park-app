# Verify the live deployment

After Vercel reports the deployment as Ready:

1. Open the exact URL shown on that Vercel deployment card.
2. Scroll to the footer or open **More**. Confirm **build 2026.07.14.4**.
3. Open **More → App View** and choose **Park Superintendent**.
4. Open the Park Superintendent tile and confirm maintenance, notices, capacity and trends appear.
5. Return to Dog Owner view. Open Owner Duty, Puppy Socialisation, Compatibility, Heat & Hazards and Legal Centre.
6. When an older build appears, use **More → Refresh latest app version**, then reopen the site.
7. A 404 on a guessed address means that address is not the current Vercel deployment URL. Use the URL displayed by Vercel for the latest Ready deployment.
