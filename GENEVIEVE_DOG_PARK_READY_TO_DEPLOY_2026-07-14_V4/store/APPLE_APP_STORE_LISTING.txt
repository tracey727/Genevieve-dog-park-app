GENEVIEVE Dog Park

SUBTITLE
Safer dog park planning

PROMOTIONAL TEXT
Plan visits, check dog heat risk, compare profiles and park conditions, and manage best-mate alerts.

KEYWORDS
dog park,dog profile,pet safety,heat safety,off leash,dog compatibility,pet alerts

DESCRIPTION
GENEVIEVE App™ Dog Park helps dog owners prepare for dog park visits with owner-controlled dog profiles, park information, dog heat-risk checks, interaction-risk decision support, check-in tools and privacy controls.

Select two dog profiles and a park to review a graduated interaction-risk result with written reasons and environmental adjustments. Use the Heat Safety screen with manual conditions or optional current-weather retrieval. Record outcomes, manage best-mate relationships and choose whether presence or alerts are visible.

Features may include:
• owner-controlled dog profiles
• behavioural interaction-risk guidance
• dog-specific heat-risk checks with manual weather entry
• prospective companion recommendations
• preferred-park best-mate alerts
• check-in and check-out
• one consistent 0–100 risk direction, with red only at the high end
• labelled green, yellow, amber and red guidance
• privacy, export and account-deletion controls
• park-planning and owner-supervision reminders

Important: GENEVIEVE App™ supports safer decisions but does not guarantee behaviour, compatibility, heat safety, weather accuracy, availability, legal compliance or data accuracy. Owners remain responsible for their dogs and must follow official rules, signs and emergency directions.

Some features require an auto-renewing subscription. The subscription period, local price, trial terms, included access, automatic renewal and cancellation method are displayed before purchase.
