Respect people and animals

  • Supervise and control your dog; do not leave the dog unattended.
  • Keep leads on where required and use gates correctly.
  • Ask before approaching or allowing dogs to greet.
  • Respect needs-space, on-lead, training, puppy, senior and disability-related information.
  • Do not harass, shame, threaten or publish private information.
  • Make honest hazard, incident and lost/found reports.
  • Do not use the app to decide legal liability or encourage unsafe confrontation.

Moderation

Unsafe, abusive, deceptive or unlawful content may be restricted or removed, and accounts may be suspended.
