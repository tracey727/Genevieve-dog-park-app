Products

The working catalogue contains Standard Monthly, Concession Monthly, Standard Annual and Concession Annual subscriptions. The final store or checkout screen controls the actual price and billing period.

Trial and renewal

Where offered, 30 days may be free for eligible new subscribers. After the trial, the selected price is charged each month or year and renews automatically until cancelled. Cancel before the trial ends to avoid the first charge.

Billing channel

Web subscriptions are managed through the configured web payment provider. Apple subscriptions are managed through Apple. Google Play subscriptions are managed through Google Play. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

Concession products

Concession eligibility, verification, review and privacy handling must be finalised before those products are sold.

Entitlements

Production premium access requires secure server-side verification and restoration of purchases. The static web demonstration does not by itself provide cross-device entitlement verification.
