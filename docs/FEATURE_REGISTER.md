# Complete Restored Feature Register

## Main user flow

- Today: one risk answer, live snapshot and one-tap dog status.
- Journey: before leaving, route, lead-on path, gate assessment, check-in and checkout.
- Parks: search, alphabetical needs, details, live state and dog beaches.
- Dogs: full profiles, Mr Gruff, puppy socialisation, compatibility, companions and best mates.
- More: Owner Duty, etiquette, heat/hazards, travel, emergency, lost/found, incidents, notices, roles, superintendent, notifications, membership, settings, legal, data and launch check.

## Restored operational modules

- Lead from vehicle to signed off-leash boundary and lead again before leaving.
- Owner-supervision confirmation and unattended-dog reports.
- Park superintendent/council operator maintenance and notice view.
- Dog/public/behavioural/restricted-emergency profile separation.
- Owner-only document-expiry reminders.
- Puppy life stage, veterinary/vaccination clearance and controlled socialisation matching.
- Behaviour-based matching rather than breed-only matching.
- Pair and group interaction-risk engine.
- Prospective companion recommendations.
- Mutual and one-way affinity links.
- Preferred-park alerts and incognito controls.
- Heat, surface, shade, water and vulnerability risk.
- Community hazard reports.
- Council etiquette/body-language prompts and recorded break actions.
- Grey Nomad/trip planning.
- Emergency summary, vet search and lone-walker timer.
- Lost/found and incident records.
- Role-based view labels.
- Four-product payment router.
- Privacy, legal, export, correction and deletion.
- Patent evidence export.
