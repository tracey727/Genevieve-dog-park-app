## GenevieveBoardingKennels bolt-on decision
A future Home Boarding Match mode is technically compatible with the existing pet profile and compatibility engine. It must be treated as a separate two-sided marketplace layer with host verification, property verification, insurance, payment handling, cancellation/refund rules, reviews, incident management, dispute handling, privacy controls and legal review. It is recorded in V11 but not activated for public bookings so the Dog Park launch remains the active priority.
