APP NAME
GENEVIEVE Dog Park

SHORT DESCRIPTION
Plan dog park visits with heat checks, profiles, alerts and interaction-risk guidance.

FULL DESCRIPTION
GENEVIEVE App™ Dog Park helps dog owners prepare for dog park visits using owner-controlled dog profiles, park information, check-in tools and clearly labelled decision-support prompts.

Choose a dog and park to review available information about fencing, capacity, water, shade, access, crowding, current needs and precautionary heat risk. Compare behavioural profiles, record interaction outcomes, manage best-mate relationships and control visibility and notifications.

Features may include:
• owner-controlled dog profiles
• behavioural interaction-risk decision support
• dog-specific heat-risk checks with manual weather entry
• prospective companion recommendations
• best-mate relationships and preferred-park alerts
• check-in and check-out tools
• one consistent 0–100 risk direction: green at the lower end and red at the highest end
• green, yellow, amber and red guidance with written reasons
• privacy, export and deletion controls
• park-planning and safety reminders

GENEVIEVE App™ supports safer decisions but does not guarantee animal behaviour, compatibility, heat safety, weather accuracy, park safety, availability, legal compliance or the accuracy of user-supplied or third-party information. Owners remain responsible for direct supervision, effective control and checking current council signs and official information.

Some features require an auto-renewing subscription. Price, billing period, trial, renewal and cancellation details are shown before purchase.
